# Privacy Policy for X Lobster Hat

**Last Updated: March 13, 2026**

X Lobster Hat (the "Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

## 1. No Data Collection
The Extension **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data, browsing history, or sensitive information to external servers. All operations are performed locally on your device.

## 2. Local Storage
The Extension uses `chrome.storage.local` to store:
- User-defined settings (e.g., hat colors, custom SVG paths).
- Historical mention counts and leaderboard data associated with X (Twitter) usernames.

This data remains on your device and is not shared with the developer or any third parties.

## 3. Usage of Permissions
- **storage**: Used to save your preferences and leaderboard scores locally.
- **tabs**: Used to implement the "Share" feature and update the extension icon badge.
- **x.com Access**: Used solely to detect specific keywords in tweets and render visual overlays on user avatars. No private data (DMs, credentials) is ever read or accessed.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The Extension interacts with X (Twitter) only to modify the visual presentation of the website for the user. It does not communicate with any other third-party services.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
